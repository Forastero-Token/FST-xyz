Forastero Ecosystem

The Forastero Token (FST) Ecosystem integrates blockchain technology, decentralized finance (DeFi), and real-world cocoa farming to create a sustainable, transparent, and profitable model for investors, farmers, and stakeholders.

‚úÖ Cocoa-Backed Tokenization

Forastero Token (FST) is directly tied to cocoa production, ensuring that 1 FST represents 1 kg of cocoa. The ecosystem will start with 10 million cocoa trees, matching the total supply of 10,000,000 FST tokens.

‚úÖ Smart Contract Automation

Smart contracts power staking rewards, revenue sharing, and governance, ensuring secure, immutable transactions without intermediaries.

‚úÖ Decentralized Staking & Rewards

Instead of burning, Forastero Token implements a buyback strategy where repurchased tokens will be kept in a staking pool to reward long-term investors. This ensures sustained yield for stakeholders.

‚úÖ Yield & Production

Cocoa yields range between 2kg to 5kg per tree. With the projected 10 million cocoa trees, the ecosystem will generate significant value and token-backed production scalability.

‚úÖ Market Expansion & Trading Utility

FST will be listed on major exchanges, enabling trading, liquidity farming, and cross-border cocoa transactions with real-time blockchain settlements.

‚úÖ Fair Token Launch & Pricing

The pre-sale price is set at an affordable $1.50 equivalent to STX per FST token., offering early investors a valuable entry opportunity. Upon market launch, the token price will be pegged to the real cocoa price per kg at that time, ensuring intrinsic value.

‚úÖ Long-Term Price Growth

Our vision is to grow the token price into the $100+ range once the buyback mechanism is fully implemented. The minimum expected price at launch is set at $10 per token, based on today‚Äôs cocoa market value.

As the project scales, the value of 1 FST will evolve:

  • Over time, 1 FST will transition to representing an entire cocoa tree, with an estimated value of $100 per FST.
  • With continued growth and increased market demand, the next phase will see 1 FST representing 1 metric ton of cocoa, with a projected value exceeding $10,000 per FST.

This progressive valuation model ensures that Forastero Token holders benefit from long-term sustainability, price appreciation, and real-world agricultural productivity. The token serves as a store of value and a bridge between traditional cocoa farming and blockchain-powered asset ownership.

‚úÖ Staking & Buyback Mechanism

Forastero Token will conduct a buyback of 50% of distributed tokens using profits from cocoa sales. The buyback price per token is set at a minimum of $10, ensuring a stable valuation and strong demand. Instead of burning, these tokens will be held in the staking pool to reward long-term investors. This ensures sustained yield for stakeholders and continuous price growth, with a target value of $100+ per token as the ecosystem matures.

‚úÖ Forastero DAO (Decentralized Governance)

FST holders will have voting rights to influence farm expansions, reinvestment strategies, and ecosystem improvements.

‚úÖ Web3 Integration for Global Trade

The platform will integrate Web3 payment solutions, enabling international buyers, exporters, and investors to settle transactions in FST.

‚úÖ Farm Management & Location

The Forastero acquired farmland is located a few kilometers away from formerly proposed Edumanom National Forest Reserve , situated between Ogbia and Nembe local governments in Bayelsa State, South-South Nigeria, West Africa. The land has fertile soil ideal for cocoa cultivation, with plans to expand into the Edumanom Forest Reserve, a freshwater swamp forest covering 9,324 hectares.

‚úÖ Transparency & Verification

To ensure full transparency, we will implement 24/7 live surveillance on the farms, accessible via YouTube live streaming. This allows token holders and stakers to monitor real-time progress and verify operations.
